Overview

Purpose

My website is the best for any wedding. It helps to motivate and encourage anyone getting married and teaches them how registry is made and all-important information they need to know about wedding. My website has designs personalized online sales funnels for any wedding reception. That means two things: one, online customers enjoy a flawless user experience tailored to their needs and interests, and two: my clients get automated solutions that dramatically boost sales. My website has helped most of my clients to increase online revenue by 120% month-on-month. Does your company have any experience wedding websites?

Audience

This website will target all age groups but focus more on the youth and elderly. They make more money and will able to afford wedding events and any other charge that we issue. They are more matured and will develop affection for each other. They like fun activities like having family get together and wedding receptions and can invite more friends to join them. Since my audience will access the website mostly through their laptops, our content has some amazing pictures that will keep the audience motivated to join our wedding website. The website also has some ralaxing and chilling pictures for the youth and elderly audience to feel exited about their new journey in life. The colors and fonts are attractive and apealing to the eyes which will invite more people to visit and learn from the website.
